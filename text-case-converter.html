What is a Text Case Converter?

A text case converter is an essential online tool that instantly changes the capitalization style of any text. Whether you need to convert text to uppercase, convert text to lowercase, or use a title case converter online, our free tool makes formatting quick and effortless. This uppercase lowercase converter is perfect for content writers, bloggers, students, SEO professionals, and anyone who works with text regularly.

Example: "hello world" → "HELLO WORLD" (UPPERCASE)

"HELLO WORLD" → "hello world" (lowercase)

"hello world" → "Hello World" (Title Case)

Types of Case Conversion

  • 🔠 UPPERCASE – All letters to capitals
  • 🔡 lowercase – All letters to small letters
  • 📘 Title Case – Capitalizes first letter of every word
  • 📖 Sentence case – Capitalizes first letter of each sentence
  • 📌 Capitalize Each Word – Similar to title case but more aggressive

How to Use

  1. Type or paste your text into the text box
  2. Click on your desired case conversion option
  3. The text will be instantly converted
  4. Copy the formatted text and use it anywhere

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Is this text case converter free?
Yes, completely free with unlimited usage.

❓ Does it store my text?
No, all processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never saved or shared.

❓ What is the difference between title case and sentence case?
Title case capitalizes every word; sentence case capitalizes only the first word of each sentence.

❓ Can I use this on mobile?
