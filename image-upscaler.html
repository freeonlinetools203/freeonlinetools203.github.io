🎯 What is an Image Upscaler?

An image upscaler is a tool that enlarges images while maintaining quality. Our upscaler supports 2x and 4x scaling, perfect for upscaling images to 4k, 8k, and 12k resolution. Whether you need to upscale image quality for printing, display, or design, our free tool delivers instant results.

📊 Popular Upscaling Factors

  • 2x Upscaler: Double the original dimensions
  • 4x Upscaler: Quadruple the original dimensions
  • Upscale to 4k: Perfect for HD displays
  • Upscale to 8k: For ultra-high resolution
  • Upscale to 12k: Maximum quality enlargement

🎯 How to Upscale an Image

  • Step 1: Upload your image (PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP)
  • Step 2: Select upscale factor (2x or 4x)
  • Step 3: Click "Upscale Image"
  • Step 4: Compare original and upscaled images
  • Step 5: Download your enlarged image
