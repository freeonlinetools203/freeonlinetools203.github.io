🐰 About Rabbit Pregnancy

This rabbit pregnancy calculator helps breeders estimate the kindling (birth) date. The average gestation period for rabbits is approximately 31 days (4 weeks), but can vary between 28-35 days depending on breed and other factors.

📊 Rabbit Pregnancy Stages (Week by Week)

  • Week 1 (Days 1-7): Fertilization occurs. No visible signs.
  • Week 2 (Days 8-14): Gentle palpation can detect fetuses. Increased appetite.
  • Week 3 (Days 15-21): Belly begins to enlarge. Doe may start nesting behavior.
  • Week 4 (Days 22-31): Significant weight gain. Pulling fur for nest. Birth approaches!

🏠 Signs Your Rabbit is About to Give Birth (Kindling)

  • ✅ Pulling fur from her chest and belly (24-48 hours before)
  • ✅ Building a nest with hay and fur
  • ✅ Restlessness and digging behavior
  • ✅ Loss of appetite 12-24 hours before
  • ✅ Enlarged nipples

⚠️ Important Notes

Rabbit gestation is very short (only 4 weeks!). Provide a nest box 3-4 days before the due date. Does can become pregnant again immediately after giving birth. Always consult a veterinarian for proper care.
