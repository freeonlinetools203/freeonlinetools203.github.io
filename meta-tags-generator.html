📝 Types of Meta Tags You Need

Before using a meta tags generator, it's important to understand what each tag does. A comprehensive meta tag generator should create all these essential tags:

1. Meta Title (Title Tag)

The title tag is the clickable headline in search results. It should be 50-60 characters and include your primary keyword near the beginning. A good meta tag title generator will suggest optimal title lengths.

2. Meta Description

This is the short description under your title in search results. Keep it between 150-160 characters and include a call-to-action. A keywords meta tag generator online will help you naturally include your keywords.

3. Meta Keywords

While Google doesn't use meta keywords for ranking, other search engines like Bing and Yandex still consider them. A meta tag keyword generator helps you create relevant keyword lists.

4. Open Graph Tags (Facebook & LinkedIn)

These tags control how your page looks when shared on social media. They include og:title, og:description, and og:image. A complete seo meta tags generator always includes Open Graph tags.

5. Twitter Cards

Similar to Open Graph, Twitter Cards control how your page appears on Twitter. The "summary_large_image" card type typically performs best.

6. Canonical Tag

This tag prevents duplicate content issues by telling search engines which URL is the master copy. Every good meta tag generator includes canonical tag generation.

7. Robots Meta Tag

This tag tells search engines how to crawl and index your page. Use "index, follow" for pages you want in search results.

📝 Meta Title Best Practices (2026 Update)

Using a meta tag title generator is helpful, but understanding best practices ensures optimal results. Here's what you need to know:

  • Length: Keep your title between 50-60 characters. Google displays about 600 pixels (50-60 characters on desktop).
  • Keyword Placement: Put your primary keyword within the first 60 characters.
  • Brand Name: Add your brand name at the end, separated by a pipe (|) or dash (-).
  • Unique Titles: Every page needs a unique meta title. Duplicate titles confuse Google.
  • Power Words: Use words like "Free", "Best", "Complete", "Guide", "2026" to increase CTR.
  • Match Search Intent: Your title should promise what the user is looking for.

🖼️ Open Graph & Twitter Cards Guide

When you share your page on social media, Open Graph and Twitter Card tags determine how it appears. A complete meta tags seo generator always includes these tags:

Open Graph Tags (Facebook, LinkedIn, WhatsApp)

  • og:title: Should match your meta title. Keep under 90 characters.
  • og:description: Should complement your meta description. Keep under 200 characters.
  • og:image: Use 1200x630 pixel images for best results.
  • og:url: The canonical URL of your page.
  • og:type: Use "website" for standard pages, "article" for blog posts.

Twitter Cards

  • twitter:card: Use "summary_large_image" for best results.
  • twitter:title: Should match your og:title.
  • twitter:description: Should match your og:description.
  • twitter:image: Use the same image as og:image.

🚨 10 Common Meta Tag Mistakes (And How to Fix Them)

  • Missing Meta Title: Always add a unique title tag to every page.
  • Duplicate Meta Titles: Use an SEO plugin to enforce unique titles.
  • Title Too Long (>60 chars): Move brand name to the end or truncate.
  • Title Too Short (<30 chars): Add descriptive words or your brand name.
  • Missing Meta Description: Write a unique description for every important page.
  • Duplicate Meta Descriptions: Customize each description to match the page content.
  • Missing Open Graph Tags: Add og:title, og:description, and og:image tags.
  • Missing Twitter Cards: Add twitter:card and twitter:image tags.
  • Missing Canonical Tag: Add rel="canonical" to every page.
  • Wrong Robots Meta Tag: Use "index, follow" unless you have a specific reason not to.
