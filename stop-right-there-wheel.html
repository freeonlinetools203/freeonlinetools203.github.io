1️⃣ What is Stop Right There Game?

Stop Right There! is an exciting reflex-based party game where players spin a wheel and must react quickly when the wheel stops. The game combines elements of chance, quick reflexes, and hilarious dares. Unlike traditional wheel games where the wheel automatically stops, "Stop Right There" requires the player to press the "STOP!" button themselves – adding an extra layer of excitement and anticipation.

The phrase "stop right there" has become iconic in gaming culture, especially from the popular stop right there criminal scum meme originating from The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion. This game captures that same energy – you never know what action you'll land on when you stop the wheel!

2️⃣ The "Stop Right There Criminal Scum" Meme

The stop right there criminal scum meme originated from The Elder Scrolls IV: Oblivion (2006). In the game, guards would approach the player with the infamous line: "Stop right there, criminal scum!" This became one of the most beloved and quotable moments in gaming history.

The stop right there criminal scum gif and stop right there criminal scum quote have been shared millions of times across social media. Our game pays homage to this iconic meme with the "Extreme Edition" mode that includes dramatic guard-style actions!
