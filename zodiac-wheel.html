🎯 What is a Zodiac Wheel?

A zodiac wheel is an interactive spinning tool that randomly selects a zodiac sign – perfect for games, decision making, or daily horoscope fun. Our zodiac sign wheel offers three categories: Western zodiac, Chinese zodiac, and elemental signs. Whether you need a wheel of zodiac for party games, astrology practice, or simply to get a random zodiac sign picker, this tool is ideal.

🌟 Western Zodiac Signs (12 Signs)

The Western zodiac is based on the tropical zodiac, dividing the sky into 12 equal parts. Each sign has unique traits: ♈ Aries (Mar 21–Apr 19) – bold, ♉ Taurus – reliable, ♊ Gemini – adaptable, ♋ Cancer – nurturing, ♌ Leo – confident, ♍ Virgo – analytical, ♎ Libra – diplomatic, ♏ Scorpio – passionate, ♐ Sagittarius – adventurous, ♑ Capricorn – disciplined, ♒ Aquarius – innovative, ♓ Pisces – intuitive. Use our zodiac wheel chart to quickly see the sequence and dates.

🐉 Chinese Zodiac Wheel (12 Animals)

The chinese zodiac wheel follows a 12-year cycle: Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Goat, Monkey, Rooster, Dog, Pig. Each year is associated with one animal and its characteristics. Our chinese zodiac wheel chart helps you randomly pick an animal for games or festivals. The chinese zodiac wheels are popular during Lunar New Year and for personality tests.

🔥 Elements Wheel

The elemental zodiac wheel with elements represents Fire, Water, Air, Earth, plus Spirit and Aether for mystical use. Elements are fundamental in astrology: Fire (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius) – passion; Water (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces) – emotion; Air (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius) – intellect; Earth (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) – practicality. This wheel of the zodiac version is great for elemental balancing games.
