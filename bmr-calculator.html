BMR Calculator

Basal Metabolic Rate - calories your body burns at rest. Use this to plan your daily calorie needs for weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain.

💡 What is BMR? BMR is the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production while at rest.

📊 Your Daily Calorie Needs (TDEE)

Based on your BMR, multiply by activity level:

Sedentary: ×1.2
Light: ×1.375
Moderate: ×1.55
Heavy: ×1.725
