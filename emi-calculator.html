What is EMI and Why You Need an EMI Calculator?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan. It consists of both principal and interest components. Our free EMI calculator helps you calculate your monthly payment instantly, making it easier to plan your finances before taking any loan. Whether you're looking for a home loan EMI calculator, car loan EMI calculator, or personal loan EMI calculator, this tool provides accurate results.

EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]

Where: P = Principal, R = Monthly Interest Rate, N = Number of Months (EMI calculation formula)
