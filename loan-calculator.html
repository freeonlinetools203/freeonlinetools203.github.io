Simple Interest vs Compound Interest

Our loan interest calculator uses the simple interest formula, which is commonly used for many types of loans. Simple interest is calculated only on the principal amount, making it easier to understand and predict. For loans that use compound interest (like credit cards), the total payment would be higher. This loan amortization calculator is ideal for:

  • Personal loans from banks and financial institutions
  • Car loans with fixed interest rates
  • Education loans
  • Short-term business loans
  • Home loans with simple interest calculation

Tips to Reduce Your Loan Cost

  • 🔹 Make a larger down payment: Reduces the principal amount
  • 🔹 Choose a shorter tenure: Less time means less interest accumulation
  • 🔹 Improve your credit score: Better score = lower interest rates
  • 🔹 Compare lenders: Different banks offer different rates – use our loan repayment calculator to compare
  • 🔹 Consider prepayment: Pay extra when possible to reduce principal
  • 🔹 Negotiate interest rates: Sometimes lenders offer better rates
