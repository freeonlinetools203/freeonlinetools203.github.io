📈 YouTube Retention Tips

  • ✅ Hook viewers in first 30 seconds - promise value immediately
  • ✅ Use pattern interrupts every 60-90 seconds (visual changes, sound effects)
  • ✅ Tease what's coming next to keep curiosity high
  • ✅ End each section with a question or cliffhanger
  • ✅ Deliver on your title's promise - don't clickbait without value

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a YouTube video outline generator?

AI tool that creates structured video flow including intro, main points, engagement hooks, and calls-to-action.

Why are outlines important for videos?

Outlines keep your content organized, reduce editing time, and improve audience retention.

How long should a YouTube outline be?

Depends on video length - 5-10 points for 5-min video, 15-25 points for longer content.

Best structure for viral videos?

Hook → Problem → Solution → Proof → Call to Action → Outro with cliffhanger.

How to improve audience retention?

Strong hook, clear structure, pace changes, and delivering value throughout.
