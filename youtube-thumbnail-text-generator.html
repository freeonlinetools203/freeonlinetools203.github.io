  • Increases CTR: Well-optimized text can boost CTR by 30-50%
  • Mobile Friendly: Most viewers watch on phones - text must be readable

📝 Best Text Length for YouTube Thumbnails

  • Optimal Length: 3-7 words (20-40 characters)

  • Pro Tip: Use ALL CAPS for emphasis but sparingly
