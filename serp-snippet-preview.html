What is SERP Snippet Preview and Why It Matters?

A SERP snippet is the information Google displays for your webpage in search results – title, URL, and description. Optimizing these elements can significantly increase your click-through rate (CTR). Our free tool lets you test different variations before publishing.

Best Practices for Title Tags

  • Keep under 60 characters to avoid truncation.
  • Include primary keyword near the beginning.
  • Make it compelling and unique.

Meta Description Tips

  • 150-160 characters is ideal.
  • Include a call-to-action (e.g., "Learn more", "Try free").
  • Match user intent.
