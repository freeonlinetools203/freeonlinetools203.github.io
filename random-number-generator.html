What is a Random Number Generator?

A random number generator (RNG) is a tool that produces numbers that cannot be reasonably predicted better than by random chance. Our random number generator uses a secure algorithm to generate truly random numbers between any minimum and maximum values you specify. Whether you need a random number between 1 and 100 for a classroom activity, a random number between 1 and 10 for a game, or a random number between 1 and 1000 for a raffle, our tool delivers instant, fair results.
