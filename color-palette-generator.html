📊 2. Popular Color Palette Types

Our color palette generator supports multiple palette types based on color theory:

  • Monochromatic: Different shades of a single color - clean and elegant
  • Analogous: Colors next to each other on the color wheel - harmonious and calm
  • Complementary: Opposite colors on the color wheel - high contrast and vibrant
  • Triadic: Three evenly spaced colors - balanced and rich
  • Tetradic: Four colors forming a rectangle - diverse and complex
  • Square: Four colors evenly spaced on the color wheel

♿ 9. Color Accessibility Tips

Using a color blind friendly palette generator ensures your designs are accessible:

  • High Contrast: Ensure text is readable against backgrounds
  • Avoid Red-Green Combinations: Most common color blindness
  • Use Patterns: Combine color with patterns or icons
  • Test Accessibility: Use our color blind friendly mode
  • Provide Alternatives: Use text labels alongside colors
  • Follow WCAG Guidelines: Contrast ratio of 4.5:1 minimum
