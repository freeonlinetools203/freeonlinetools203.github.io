How YouTube Thumbnails Work

YouTube automatically generates thumbnails for every video using frames from the video. However, creators can upload custom thumbnails for better click-through rates. YouTube stores these thumbnails at different resolutions on their servers. Our youtube thumbnails downloader tool accesses these server URLs to fetch the original images. The youtube thumbnail viewer and downloader functionality allows you to preview before downloading.

The highest quality thumbnail (maxresdefault.jpg) is only available for videos that have HD/4K enabled and have a custom thumbnail uploaded. If a video doesn't have a custom thumbnail or HD quality, our tool automatically falls back to the next available quality.
