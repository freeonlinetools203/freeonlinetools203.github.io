🐱 About Feline Pregnancy Calculator

This cat pregnancy calculator helps you estimate your queen's (female cat) due date based on the mating date. The average gestation period for cats is approximately 63-67 days (9-10 weeks) from the first mating. However, cats can deliver anywhere between 61-70 days depending on breed, litter size, and individual factors.

📊 Cat Pregnancy Stages Week by Week

  • Week 1-2 (Days 1-14): Fertilization occurs. No visible signs. The queen may show decreased appetite.
  • Week 3-4 (Days 15-28): Nipples become pinker and enlarged ("pinking up"). Morning sickness possible.
  • Week 5-6 (Days 29-42): Belly begins to enlarge. Vet can palpate fetuses. Appetite increases.
  • Week 7-8 (Days 43-56): Significant weight gain. Nesting behavior begins. Milk production starts.
  • Week 9-10 (Days 57-70): Queen becomes restless. Body temperature drops 24-48 hours before labor.

🏠 Signs Your Cat is About to Give Birth

  • ✅ Nesting behavior - seeking quiet, warm places
  • ✅ Drop in body temperature below 100°F (37.8°C)
  • ✅ Loss of appetite 24 hours before labor
  • ✅ Restlessness, pacing, excessive grooming
  • ✅ Milk production from nipples

⚠️ Important Notes

This calculator provides an estimated due date. Always consult your veterinarian for proper prenatal care, ultrasound confirmation, and queening assistance. Factors like breed, litter size, and the queen's health can affect the actual delivery date. If pregnancy exceeds 70 days, seek immediate veterinary care.
