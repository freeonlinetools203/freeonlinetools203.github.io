vCard QR Code for Business Cards

Enter your full name, phone number, email, website, and company. Our tool generates a vCard format (version 3.0) that is compatible with iOS and Android. When scanned, the phone will prompt to add the contact. Use it on your physical business cards, LinkedIn profile, or email signature.

Google Maps QR Code for Physical Locations

Type the address (e.g., "Statue of Liberty, New York") or GPS coordinates. The QR code encodes a Google Maps search URL. When scanned, it opens the Maps app with the location pinned. Perfect for event invitations, restaurant menus, real estate signs, and museum labels.

QR Codes for Google Forms & Sheets

Share a feedback form, registration form, or spreadsheet directly via QR code. Simply paste the shareable link from Google Forms or Google Sheets. Make sure the link permissions are set to "Anyone with the link can view" or "can edit".

Transparent Background & Color Customization

Standard QR codes have a white square background. Transparent background QR codes blend seamlessly into any design – you can overlay them on product packaging, brochures, or social media graphics. Use the checkbox to remove the white background (PNG only). For JPG, a white background is automatically added because JPG doesn't support transparency.

Export Formats: PNG, JPG, PDF

PNG is best for digital use with transparent background support. JPG is smaller file size and widely compatible, but doesn't support transparency. PDF is ideal for print – our generator creates a single A4/letter-sized page with the QR code centered, ready to print on flyers, posters, or business cards.
