Best Dares for Truth or Dare (2025 Edition)

Whether you're playing with friends, your crush, or over text, here are good dares for truth or dare that guarantee laughs:

  • Physical challenges: "Do 20 jumping jacks", "Hold a plank for 30 seconds", "Try to lick your elbow"
  • Social dares: "Send a compliment to the person on your left", "Post an old embarrassing photo", "Change your WhatsApp status to something silly"
  • Voice/acting: "Sing a nursery rhyme dramatically", "Imitate a famous person", "Say something in a foreign accent for 1 minute"
  • Texting dares: "Send a random GIF to your last chat", "Voice note – make a funny sound", "Text 'I'm thinking of you' to a friend"

What is a Good Truth for Truth or Dare?

Great truth questions should be juicy but not too invasive. Here are good truth questions for truth or dare that work for any group:

  • "What's the biggest lie you've told recently?"
  • "Have you ever had a secret crush on someone in this room?"
  • "What's your most embarrassing moment that you haven't told anyone?"
  • "What's something you're afraid to tell your parents?"
  • "What's the last text you regret sending?"

Truth or Dare Over Text – Ideas & Tips

Playing truth or dare over text is huge for long-distance friendships or flirting. Our generator includes a dedicated "Texting Dares" mode. Good text dares include: sending a voice note, posting a story, or changing your profile picture. Truth questions like "What's your average reply time?" or "Who do you text the most?" work great.

Crush Edition – Flirty Truth or Dare

If you're playing with someone you like, choose our Crush Edition. Dares like "Send a flirty emoji" or "Draw a heart with their initial" are low-risk but exciting. Truths like "What's your type?" or "What would be your perfect first date?" help you learn more about them.
