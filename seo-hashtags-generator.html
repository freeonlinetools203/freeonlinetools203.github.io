📊 Types of Hashtags

  • 🔥 Trending Hashtags: Popular tags currently being used by many users. Great for reaching new audiences.
  • 🚀 Viral Hashtags: Tags that have high potential to go viral. Often related to current events or challenges.
  • 🎯 Niche Hashtags: Specific to your industry or topic. Attract targeted, engaged audiences.
  • 📍 Location Hashtags: Based on specific locations (cities, countries, landmarks).
  • 🏷️ Branded Hashtags: Unique to your brand. Build community and brand recognition.
  • 📈 Community Hashtags: Used by specific communities or interest groups.

✅ Platform-Specific Hashtag Strategies

📸 Instagram Hashtags Strategy

  • Quantity: Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. Use 10-15 for best engagement.
  • Placement: Add hashtags in caption or first comment (both work).
  • Mix Types: Use a mix of trending, niche, and location hashtags.
  • Size Matters: Use hashtags with varying popularity (small: 10k-50k posts, medium: 50k-500k posts, large: 500k+ posts).

🎵 TikTok Hashtags Strategy

  • Quantity: Use 3-5 relevant hashtags (fewer than Instagram).
  • Trending Focus: TikTok favors trending hashtags more than any platform.
  • Niche + Broad: Combine niche-specific tags with broader trending tags.

🐦 Twitter/X Hashtags Strategy

  • Quantity: Use 1-2 hashtags per tweet (too many looks spammy).
  • Relevance: Focus on highly relevant, trending conversation tags.

💼 LinkedIn Hashtags Strategy

  • Quantity: Use 3-5 professional, industry-specific hashtags.
  • Quality: Focus on professional topics (#Marketing, #Leadership, #SEO).
