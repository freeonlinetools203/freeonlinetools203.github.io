What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator is an essential health tool that determines your daily energy needs based on your age, gender, weight, height, and activity level. It calculates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) – the calories your body burns at rest – and your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) – your total calorie burn including activity. This is the foundation of any successful weight management plan.

BMR (Mifflin-St Jeor) = (10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) - (5 × age) + 5 (male) / -161 (female)

TDEE = BMR × Activity Factor

Understanding BMR and TDEE

Your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic life-sustaining functions like breathing and circulation. Your TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) includes all physical activity. To lose weight, you need a calorie deficit; to gain weight, a calorie surplus. Our calorie deficit calculator automatically adjusts your recommended intake based on your goal.
