🎯 What is PDF to WEBP Converter?

A PDF to WEBP converter transforms PDF document pages into WEBP (Web Picture) format. WEBP is a modern image format developed by Google that offers lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. It typically produces files 25-35% smaller than JPEG while maintaining comparable or better quality.

WEBP supports both lossy (like JPEG) and lossless (like PNG) compression, as well as transparency and animation. This makes it the ideal choice for modern web development and mobile applications where file size matters.

What is WEBP Format? WEBP (pronounced "weppy") was introduced by Google in 2010. It's designed specifically for the web, providing superior compression algorithms that reduce file size without sacrificing quality. WEBP files are typically 25-35% smaller than equivalent JPG files.

📈 Understanding WEBP Quality Settings
