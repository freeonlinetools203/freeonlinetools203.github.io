What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a value-added tax levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption. The GST calculator helps businesses and individuals determine the tax amount to be paid or collected. Different countries have varying rates: India GST has slabs of 5%, 12%, 18%, and 28%; Pakistan GST is 17%; UAE VAT is 5%; UK VAT is 20%; Australia GST is 10%; Singapore GST is 9%; and Canada HST varies by province.

How to Calculate GST?

Understanding how to calculate GST is essential for businesses. The formula depends on whether you're adding or removing tax:

  • Add GST: GST Amount = (Original Amount × GST Rate) ÷ 100. Total = Original Amount + GST Amount.
  • Remove GST: Original Amount = Total Amount ÷ (1 + GST Rate/100). GST Amount = Total Amount - Original Amount.
  • Reverse GST: Original Amount = Inclusive Total ÷ (1 + GST Rate/100). GST = Inclusive Total - Original Amount.

GST Calculator India – Indian Tax System

A GST calculator India is essential for businesses registered under the Indian GST regime. India has a dual GST structure with CGST (Central) and SGST (State) for intra-state transactions, and IGST for inter-state transactions.
