Last Updated: April 13, 2026 | 8 min read | By Image Editing Experts

🎯 What is an Image Cropper?

An image cropper is a tool that allows you to remove unwanted areas from an image by selecting a specific region. Our online cropper lets you adjust aspect ratios, drag to select crop area, and download the cropped image instantly. Perfect for social media posts, thumbnails, profile pictures, and design projects.

📊 Popular Aspect Ratios

  • 1:1 (Square): Perfect for Instagram posts and profile pictures
  • 4:3 (Photo): Standard photo ratio for cameras
  • 16:9 (Widescreen): YouTube thumbnails and video backgrounds
  • 3:2 (DSLR): Professional camera photos
  • 2:3 (Portrait): Vertical photos for mobile
  • 9:16 (Story): Instagram and Facebook stories

🎯 How to Crop an Image

  • Step 1: Upload your image (PNG, JPG, JPEG, WEBP)
  • Step 2: Select aspect ratio preset or keep free
  • Step 3: Drag to select crop area on the image
  • Step 4: Click "Apply Crop" to crop
  • Step 5: Download your cropped image
