✅ Best Instagram Hashtag Strategy for 2026

  • Quantity: Instagram allows up to 30 hashtags per post. Using 15-20 relevant hashtags is recommended for best engagement.
  • Placement: Add hashtags in caption or first comment (both work effectively). For Reels, put them in the caption.
  • Mix of Sizes: Use hashtags with varying popularity - small (10k-50k posts), medium (50k-500k posts), large (500k+ posts).
  • Rotate Hashtags: Don't use the same hashtags for every post. Rotate them to reach different audiences.
  • Avoid Banned Hashtags: Some hashtags are shadowbanned. Our generator only uses safe hashtags.
  • Reels Optimization: For Reels, use 3-5 trending hashtags + niche hashtags.
