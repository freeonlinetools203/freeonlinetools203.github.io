What is Zakat?

Zakat is one of the five pillars of Islam – an obligatory form of charity. Every eligible Muslim must pay 2.5% of their surplus wealth to those in need. Our Zakat calculator helps you determine your exact obligation.

What is Nisab?

Nisab is the minimum wealth before Zakat becomes due: approximately 87.48g gold or 612.36g silver. Our calculator checks your net wealth against silver Nisab (enter the current market value of 612g silver).

How to Use

  1. Enter all assets (cash, gold, silver, investments, business assets, savings).
  2. Enter all debts and liabilities.
  3. Enter the current silver Nisab value (e.g., 612g × silver price).
  4. Click "Calculate Zakat".

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is Zakat?
Zakat is a mandatory charitable contribution in Islam, 2.5% of eligible savings.

❓ Do I pay Zakat on gold jewelry?
Yes, if it reaches Nisab and you have possessed it for one lunar year.
