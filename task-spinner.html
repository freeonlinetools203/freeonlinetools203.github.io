🎯 What is a Task Spinner?

A task spinner is an innovative productivity tool that randomly selects tasks from your to-do list using a spinning wheel. Instead of struggling with decision paralysis about what to do next, let the random task selector decide for you. It adds an element of fun and surprise to task management, making productivity more engaging.

📌 Did You Know? Studies show that random task selection can reduce decision fatigue by up to 40% and increase productivity by making task initiation more fun!
