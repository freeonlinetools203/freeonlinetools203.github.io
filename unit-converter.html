🎯 What is a Unit Converter?

A unit converter is an essential tool that converts measurements from one unit to another. Whether you need to convert metric units to imperial, or work with pressure units, our tool handles it all. Perfect for students, engineers, scientists, and anyone working with measurements.

📊 Supported Categories

  • 📐 Length: Meter, Kilometer, Centimeter, Millimeter, Mile, Yard, Foot, Inch
  • ⚖️ Weight: Kilogram, Gram, Milligram, Pound, Ounce, Ton
  • 🌡️ Temperature: Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin
  • ⚡ Speed: m/s, km/h, mph, Knot, ft/s
  • 💨 Pressure: Pascal, kPa, Bar, PSI, Atmosphere, Torr

🎯 How to Convert Units of Measurement
