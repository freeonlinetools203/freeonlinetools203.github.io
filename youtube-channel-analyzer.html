🎯 What is a YouTube Channel Analyzer?

A YouTube Channel Analyzer pulls real‑time public data from any YouTube channel using the official YouTube Data API v3. It reveals essential metrics: subscriber count, total video views, number of uploaded videos, and performance of recent videos (views, likes, and even video tags). For creators, digital marketers, and SEO specialists, this tool is indispensable for competitor research, identifying content gaps, and optimizing video strategies.

📈 Understanding the Metrics

Subscriber Count

Indicates audience size. However, engagement rate (likes/views) often matters more than raw subscriber numbers.

Total Views

Cumulative watch time across all videos – reflects overall reach and historical performance.

Video Views & Likes

High like‑to‑view ratio (≥5%) shows strong audience satisfaction.

Video Tags

Hidden by some creators, but when public they offer SEO gold. Copy and adapt them for your own videos.

📱 How to Make a YouTube Channel on Phone (Step by Step)

How to make a channel on YouTube on phone is easier than ever. Follow these steps:

  1. Download YouTube App: Install the official YouTube app from Play Store (Android) or App Store (iPhone)
  2. Sign in with Google Account: Use your existing Google account or create a new one
  3. Tap on Your Profile Picture: Located in the top-right corner of the app
  4. Tap "Your Channel": Then click "Create Channel"
  5. Add Channel Name: Enter your desired channel name (you can change it later)
  6. Add Profile Picture: Upload a photo or logo for your channel
  7. Add Channel Description: Write a short bio about your content
  8. Start Uploading: Tap the + icon to upload your first video

How to make a second channel on YouTube is also simple. Go to Settings → "See all your channels" → "Create a new channel" – all within the same Google account!

💰 How to Create a YouTube Channel and Make Money

How to create a YouTube channel and make money requires meeting YouTube's monetization requirements:

  • 1,000 Subscribers: Minimum subscriber count for YouTube Partner Program
  • 4,000 Watch Hours: Or 10 million Shorts views in 90 days
  • AdSense Account: Link your Google AdSense account for payments
  • Follow Policies: Must comply with YouTube's monetization policies
  • Multiple Income Streams: Ads, channel memberships, Super Chats, merchandise

Once approved, you can earn money from ads displayed on your videos. Popular niches like gaming, education, and tech reviews tend to earn higher RPM (Revenue Per Mille).

🎮 How to Make a Gaming YouTube Channel

How to make a gaming YouTube channel requires specific strategies:
