🐕 About Dog Pregnancy Calculator

This canine pregnancy calculator helps you estimate your dog's due date based on the breeding date. The average gestation period for dogs is approximately 63 days (9 weeks) from the first mating, but can vary between 58-68 days depending on breed and individual factors.

📊 Pregnancy Stages Week by Week

  • Weeks 1-3 (Days 1-21): Fertilization and implantation. No visible signs.
  • Weeks 4-5 (Days 22-35): Morning sickness possible. Appetite changes.
  • Weeks 5-6 (Days 36-42): Belly begins to enlarge. Nipples become pinker.
  • Weeks 7-8 (Days 43-56): Significant belly growth. Fetal movement visible.
  • Week 9 (Days 57-63): Nesting behavior. Milk production begins.

⚠️ Important Notes

This calculator provides an estimated due date. Always consult your veterinarian for proper prenatal care, ultrasound confirmation, and whelping assistance. Factors like breed, litter size, and health can affect the actual delivery date.
