What is a Date Calculator?

A date calculator is a digital tool that helps you perform date arithmetic, such as calculating the difference between two dates, adding or subtracting days, weeks, months, or years. Our date duration calculator handles all these tasks with precision, accounting for leap years, month lengths, and calendar variations.
