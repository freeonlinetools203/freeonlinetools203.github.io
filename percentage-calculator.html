Percentage Formula and Calculations

The basic percentage formula is:

Percentage Value = (Percentage × Total) ÷ 100

Common percentage calculations include:

  • Finding percentage of a number: (Percentage ÷ 100) × Number
  • Finding what percentage one number is of another: (Part ÷ Whole) × 100
  • Percentage increase: [(New Value - Old Value) ÷ Old Value] × 100
  • Percentage decrease: [(Old Value - New Value) ÷ Old Value] × 100
