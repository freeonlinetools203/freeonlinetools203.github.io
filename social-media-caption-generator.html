❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is a caption generator?

An AI-powered tool that creates engaging, platform-optimized captions based on your topic and preferred mood.

Good captions encourage likes, comments, shares, and saves - all signals that boost algorithm visibility.

Absolutely! Captions drive engagement, tell your story, and help the algorithm understand your content.
