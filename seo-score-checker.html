🎯 What is an SEO Score Checker?

An SEO Score Checker is a powerful tool that evaluates how well your website is optimized for search engines like Google, Bing, and Yahoo. It analyzes critical on-page SEO elements including title tags, meta descriptions, keyword usage, content quality, and technical factors like HTTPS. Our free website audit tool gives you an instant score from 0 to 100, along with actionable recommendations to improve your rankings.

📊 How Our SEO Score Calculator Works

Our SEO score calculator evaluates your website based on 5 essential ranking factors. Each factor contributes up to 20 points to your total score of 100:

  • 🏷️ Title Tag Optimization (0-20): Length 30-60 characters, keyword placement
  • 📄 Meta Description Quality (0-20): Length 120-160 characters, keyword inclusion
  • 🔑 Keyword Optimization (0-20): Natural keyword usage in title and meta
  • 📝 Content Quality & Length (0-20): Word count 600+ words recommended
  • ⚙️ Technical SEO (0-20): HTTPS, valid URL structure

📈 What is a Good SEO Score?

  • 80-100 (Excellent): Well-optimized page, great job!
  • 60-79 (Good): Above average, minor improvements needed
  • 40-59 (Average): Room for improvement
  • 20-39 (Poor): Significant issues detected
  • 0-19 (Critical): Major SEO problems, needs immediate attention
