🛠️ How to Make a Prize Wheel (DIY Guide)

If you prefer a physical prize spinning wheel diy, here's a quick guide:

  1. Materials: Cardboard or wood circle, spinner arrow, paint
  2. Step 1: Cut a circle (18-24 inches diameter)
  3. Step 2: Divide into equal segments
  4. Step 3: Paint each segment with prize labels
  5. Step 4: Attach a spinner arrow in the center
  6. Step 5: Mount on a stand or wall
