📊 What is Profit/Loss Calculator?

A profit/loss calculator is an essential tool for businesses, retailers, and individuals to determine the financial outcome of a transaction. By entering the cost price, selling price, and quantity, you can instantly calculate whether you made a profit or incurred a loss.

🎯 How to Use This Tool

  • Step 1: Enter the Cost Price per unit
  • Step 2: Enter the Selling Price per unit
  • Step 3: Enter the Quantity of units sold
  • Step 4: Click "Calculate Now" to see your results
  • Step 5: Use Target Profit Mode to find required selling price for desired profit %

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the formula for profit percentage?

Profit % = (Profit / Cost Price) × 100

What is the formula for loss percentage?

Loss % = (Loss / Cost Price) × 100
