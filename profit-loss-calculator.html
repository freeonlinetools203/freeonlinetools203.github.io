📊 What is Profit/Loss Calculator?

A profit/loss calculator is an essential tool for businesses, retailers, and individuals to determine the financial outcome of a transaction. By entering the cost price, selling price, and quantity, you can instantly calculate whether you made a profit or incurred a loss.
