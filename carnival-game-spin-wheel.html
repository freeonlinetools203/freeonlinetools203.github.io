🎯 What is the Carnival Game Spin Wheel?

The Carnival Game Spin Wheel is an interactive digital tool that randomly selects carnival and fairground games for events, parties, and festivals. From classic games like Ring Toss and Balloon Darts to prize games like Wheel of Fortune and food games like Cake Walk, our wheel brings the carnival experience to your fingertips.

📌 Did You Know? The first carnival games date back to medieval European fairs in the 12th century! Ring toss was originally played with horseshoes and wooden pegs.
