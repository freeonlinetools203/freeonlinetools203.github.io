How the Spin Wheel Algorithm Works

Unlike simplistic random pickers, our wheel uses a physics-inspired simulation. When you click SPIN, the wheel gains a random amount of angular momentum, spinning between 8 to 13 full rotations. A custom ease-out curve slows it down smoothly. The final resting position is mapped to the fixed arrow pointer at the top. Because the starting angle and rotational force are randomized each time, every outcome is completely unpredictable and unbiased. The winner is instantly displayed, accompanied by festive confetti.
