🐐 About Goat Pregnancy

This goat pregnancy calculator helps farmers and goat owners estimate the kidding (birth) date. The average gestation period for goats is approximately 150 days (5 months), but can vary between 145-155 days depending on breed, age, and other factors.

📊 Goat Pregnancy Stages (Month by Month)

  • Month 1 (Days 1-30): Fertilization occurs. No visible signs. Vet confirmation possible.
  • Month 2 (Days 31-60): Increased appetite. Doe may show mild behavior changes.
  • Month 3 (Days 61-90): Belly begins to enlarge. Udder development starts.
  • Month 4 (Days 91-120): Significant weight gain. Fetal movement visible.
  • Month 5 (Days 121-150): Udder fills with milk. Ligaments relax. Kidding approaches!

🏠 Signs Your Goat is About to Give Birth (Kidding)

  • ✅ Udder becomes full and tight (1-2 weeks before)
  • ✅ Relaxation of pelvic ligaments (12-24 hours before)
  • ✅ Restlessness, pawing ground, separating from herd
  • ✅ Loss of appetite 12-24 hours before kidding
  • ✅ Clear mucus discharge from vulva
  • ✅ Tail head becomes sunken

⚠️ Important Notes

This calculator provides an estimated due date. Always consult your veterinarian for proper prenatal care, ultrasound confirmation, and kidding assistance. Provide a clean, dry kidding area 2-3 weeks before the due date. Have supplies ready: iodine, towels, heat lamp, and vet contact number.
