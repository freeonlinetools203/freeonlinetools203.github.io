📈 Thumbnail Design Tips

  • ✅ Use complementary colors (opposite on color wheel) for maximum contrast
  • ✅ Red, yellow, and green increase CTR by 30-50%
  • ✅ Dark backgrounds make bright text pop - use #000000 or #1a1a2e
  • ✅ Avoid blue text on blue backgrounds - poor visibility on mobile
  • ✅ Test 2-3 color variants before publishing your video
