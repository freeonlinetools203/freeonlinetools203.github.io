🎯 What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator is a financial tool that helps you estimate the returns on your Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) investments in mutual funds. Whether you're using an SBI SIP calculator, HDFC SIP calculator, or ICICI SIP calculator, the underlying formula remains the same. Our calculator helps you plan your investments for goals like retirement, children's education, or buying a home.

📊 How SIP Returns Are Calculated

The formula used for SIP calculation is based on the future value of a series of periodic payments:

  • FV = P × ((1 + r)^n - 1) / r × (1 + r)
  • Where P = Monthly investment, r = Monthly return rate, n = Number of months

📈 Step-Up SIP Calculator

A step-up SIP calculator allows you to increase your monthly investment amount annually. This is ideal for investors whose income grows over time. For example, if you start with ₹5,000 per month and increase by 10% every year, your wealth creation potential increases significantly.

📉 SIP Calculator with Inflation

Our SIP calculator with inflation adjusts your future corpus for inflation, giving you the real value of your investments in today's terms. This helps you understand whether your investment will meet your future financial goals.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is a SIP calculator?
A SIP calculator is a financial tool that estimates the returns on your Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) investments in mutual funds based on expected annual returns and investment period.
❓ How to calculate SIP returns?
Enter your monthly investment amount, expected return rate, and investment duration. Our SIP calculator will instantly show your estimated maturity amount and total returns.
❓ What is step-up SIP?
Step-up SIP allows you to increase your monthly investment amount annually. This helps you invest more as your income grows, potentially leading to higher returns.

❓ What is a good expected return for SIP?
For equity mutual funds, expected returns are typically 10-15% per annum. For debt funds, 6-8% per annum. Past performance doesn't guarantee future returns.
