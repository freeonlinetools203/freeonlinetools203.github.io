🎯 What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator is a financial tool that helps you estimate the returns on your Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) investments in mutual funds. Whether you're using an SBI SIP calculator, HDFC SIP calculator, or ICICI SIP calculator, the underlying formula remains the same. Our calculator helps you plan your investments for goals like retirement, children's education, or buying a home.

📊 How SIP Returns Are Calculated

The formula used for SIP calculation is based on the future value of a series of periodic payments:

  • FV = P × ((1 + r)^n - 1) / r × (1 + r)
  • Where P = Monthly investment, r = Monthly return rate, n = Number of months

📈 Step-Up SIP Calculator

A step-up SIP calculator allows you to increase your monthly investment amount annually. This is ideal for investors whose income grows over time. For example, if you start with ₹5,000 per month and increase by 10% every year, your wealth creation potential increases significantly.

📉 SIP Calculator with Inflation
