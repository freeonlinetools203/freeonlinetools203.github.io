What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator is a financial tool that estimates the future value of your monthly investments in mutual funds. It uses the power of compounding to show how your regular investments grow over time. Our systematic investment plan calculator supports multiple currencies including PKR, INR, USD, GBP, EUR, AED, SGD, CAD, AUD, JPY, and CNY.

How to Calculate SIP Returns?

The SIP return formula is based on compound interest: Future Value = P × ((1 + r)^n - 1) / r × (1 + r), where P is monthly investment, r is monthly rate of return, and n is number of months. Our SIP return calculator does this automatically for you.
