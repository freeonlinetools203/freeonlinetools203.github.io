🔥 Do Hashtags Work on YouTube?

Do hashtags work on YouTube? Absolutely! YouTube introduced hashtag support in 2018. When you add hashtags to your video title or description, they become clickable links that help users discover related content.

📈 Best Hashtags for YouTube - Complete Breakdown

1. Trending Hashtags

Trending hashtags give your content an immediate visibility boost. Examples: #trending, #viral, #fyp.

2. Viral Hashtags

Viral hashtags help your content reach a massive audience. Examples: #viralvideo, #trendingnow.

3. YouTube Shorts Hashtags

Best YouTube Shorts hashtags: #Shorts, #YouTubeShorts, #ShortsFeed + niche keywords.

4. SEO Hashtags

SEO hashtags help YouTube's search algorithm understand your content. Examples: #howto, #tutorial, #guide.

📌 Where to Put Hashtags on YouTube

  • Video Title: Place 1-2 important hashtags
  • Video Description (First 3-5 lines): Most important place
  • Video Tags Section: Add all hashtags in YouTube Studio

🎯 How Many Hashtags Should I Use on YouTube?

  • Optimal Range: 5-10 hashtags per video
  • Minimum: 3 hashtags
  • Maximum: 15 hashtags (YouTube may ignore all if you exceed)
  • For Shorts: 3-5 hashtags including #Shorts

✅ How to Add Hashtags to YouTube Shorts

  1. Upload your Short as usual
  2. Add #Shorts in the title or first line of description
  3. Add 2-4 additional hashtags in description
  4. Add all hashtags in YouTube Studio's tags field
